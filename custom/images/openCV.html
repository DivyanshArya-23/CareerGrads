    About the Programme/Course

    This online course for Computer Vision with Python and OpenCV by CareerGrads is one of its kinds. The course contains more than 100 live lectures and small tutorial projects, with a full and comprehensive course curriculum. OpenCV live course begins with basics of computer vision & openCV, gradually enters the concepts of Object detection & object tracking, and finally enters into demonstrated projects. With our focus as live projects, we delve deeper into the concepts of Image manipulation and image segmentation, enabling students to develop solutions to real-world problems. It is just a matter of a few weeks before students begin to build intelligent systems, work on Augmented Reality, and bring magic to the world. As part of these online learning sections, a comprehensive overview of Python NumPy arrays and image manipulation will be integrated with students so that they can understand the concepts of computer vision quickly and efficiently.

    What you'll learn
    1. Numerical processing with the NumPy library.
    2. Image manipulation and image processing with NumPy.
    3. Understand video basics with openCV and learn object detection, face detection, object tracking, and much more.
    4. Acquire deep knowledge of computer vision and related skills to move into various fields like augmented reality, Machine Learning, etc.

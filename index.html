What is CareerGrads ?
Careergrads is a Learning Platform with in-demand trainings, courses, projects and free articles.
How do we do it ?
We do it by analysing the current industrial trends and the jobs in demand to curate courses/trainings that help students achieve their goal.
Why do we do it ?
We do it because we aim to bridge the gap between Industries and Educational Organisations while helping students realise their true potential and generate efforts in the right direction for their career.
